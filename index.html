About The 1975

The 1975 are a british dance-rock band that were formed in Mancest, England in early 2002. They covered punk rock songs and then began to explore their own style and slowly developed into what they are today. Although they have only released four albums, they have released countless singles that have been number one hits in the UK and sometimes in the US.
